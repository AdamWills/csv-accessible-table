CSV to Accessible Tables

Have an spreadsheet that you need to quickly convert to some clean, accessible HTML tables? Then you've come to the right place!

What is this?

This tool arose from a need, as do most tools. We have staff that needs to create accessible tables without knowing the details of HTML. However, there is much more familiarity with CSV files. So, why not use them to generate some accessible HTML tables?

Thanks to the folks who created jquery-csv for making my job a lot easier!
