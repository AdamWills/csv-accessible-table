What is a caption and do I need one?

Data tables very often have brief descriptive text before or after the table that indicates the content of that table. While it is not necessary for each table to have a caption, a caption is generally very helpful.

What is this?

As with most tools, HTML Table Generator arose from a need. How can clients who manage content themselves create semantic and accessible HTML tables without knowing any HTML? Most people have a much higher familiarity with Excel and can quickly create a CSV file, so, why not use them to generate some accessible HTML tables?

Thanks to the folks who created jquery-csv for making my job a lot easier!
